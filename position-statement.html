Episode I

POSITION STATEMENT

In a galaxy not so far away, a young engineer embarks on a journey to define their purpose. Guided by creativity and determination, this mission begins now.

Position Statement

The Force is strong with this engineer. Explore the ideas, inspirations, and goals driving his engineering mission.

Original Position Statement

Engineering Design is a process of slowly building "brick by brick" to achieve a certain goal, whether this be for entertainment, or to better humanity. With each problem there are various ways to approach the solution, but in the end there is one optimal way to solve it.

Updated Position Statement

Engineering Design is the iterative process of working towards a solution, with various levels of diverging and converging. With each unique opinion comes a new approach to a solution, and this can be extremely beneficial when observing the entire design space for a solution. Through various methods of validation and verification we can eventually converge onto one optimal solution.
